Building a snake game:
1. Create snake body
2. Move the snake
3. Control the snake
4. Detect food
5. Scoreboard
6. Collision with wall and tail 

main.py controls a game 
snake.py create snake, snake movement and snake growth
food.py create food on random locations 
scoreboard.py keep track of your score adn if game is over
collision.py keeps you in walls and detecting did you bite your tail
